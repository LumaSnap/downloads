# Privacy

LumaSnap records, edits, downloads, and copies videos locally on your Windows device.

The current public build does not include accounts, cloud sync, analytics, telemetry, advertising SDKs, or upload logic.

Temporary recording files are stored locally under:

```text
%LOCALAPPDATA%\LumaSnap\temp
```

Hotkey settings are stored locally under:

```text
%LOCALAPPDATA%\LumaSnap\settings.json
```
